Full description:
---------------------------------------------------------------
World Shape is a geography quiz built entirely around country outlines.

No borders, no labels, no map context — just a country's true shape, drawn
from real geographic boundary data and simplified for clarity. Pick the
right country from four choices. Wrong guesses are marked so you can try
again; your star rating depends on how many guesses it took.

FEATURES
• 91 puzzles across three atlases
   – Familiar Shores (iconic, instantly recognizable)
   – Uncharted (well-known, takes a moment)
   – Deep Atlas (for true geography buffs)
• Shapes built from real-world boundary data, not approximations
• Multiple-choice answers drawn from the same region for a genuine
  challenge — no trivially obvious mismatches
• Earn up to three stars per puzzle for guessing right the first time
• Antique-atlas visuals: parchment World Shape board, brass accents
• Calm ambient soundtrack and tactile feedback — both toggleable
• Fully offline. No ads, no accounts, no tracking. Progress stays on your
  device.
